Flame Coffee Shop Database System

november 2024

A database system designed to manage daily operations at a coffee shop, focusing on two types of users: Cashiers and Food Preparers. The system allows cashiers to process customer orders, record payments, and generate invoices, while food preparers manage order preparation and update the completion status. Each invoice includes multiple items with quantities, total price, tax, and payment method, ensuring accurate and efficient operations. Technologies: SQL, MySQL, Database Design.
